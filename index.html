Continuous Market Scanning

Our framework processes market indicators, listing portals, and proprietary agent networks. For any historic estate, the following factors are scrutinized:

  • Zoning Integrity: Checking PLU/zoning constraints for commercial hotel/vineyard potential.
  • Energy Standards: Factoring in EPC upgrade capital expenditures (converting E/G targets).
  • Agricultural Yields: AOP status assessment (Corbières-Boutenac vs standard IGP).
  • Historical Valuation: Multi-epoch historical assets valuation premiums.
